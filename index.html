ABOUT

The BrainTrip project is attempting to use quantitative analysis of EEG data to discover novel computational biomarkers for neurological and psychiatric conditions. We've performed several hundred EEG recordings using a 24-channel EEG amplifier and saline sponge based EEG caps. Our EEG data is encoded in the BrainVision format. Each EEG recoring has 3 associated files: a binary raw EEG file (.egg), and two ascii files containing marker (.vmrk) and header (.vhdr) information about special events in the EEG (markers) and channel names and positions (header). Further information on BrainVision format are available here To prepare your EEG data for upload, please convert your raw files to the BrainVison format for ease of use across different labs. Then follow the upload instructions.
